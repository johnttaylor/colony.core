25 /** This concrete class implements a blocking Receive Stream UART driver. What
26  does 'blocking Transmit Stream' mean? 'Blocking' means that the calling
27  thread will block until at least one new byte has been received. 'Stream'
28  means that data is received as a continual stream of bytes, i.e. the data is
29  NOT broken down into frames, packets, etc. (think C stdin stream).
30 
31  This driver ASSUMES that at most there is only ONE client attempt to use the
32  driver at any given time. It is okay to call the start(), stop(), read()
33  from different threads - but the calls CANNOT be concurrent. It is the
34  application's responsibility to provide additional
35  thread-safety/concurrence protection.
36  */
